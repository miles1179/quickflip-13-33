The Name, The Story

A Name That
Carries Everything.

Thirteen 33 Hair Salon was founded by Ashley Calandro in June 2024, right in the center of Wallingford, the town she grew up in.

The name is a tribute to her late father. Her initials are AC, representing the first and third letters of the alphabet: 13. His initials were CC, representing the third letter repeated: 33. Together, they are Thirteen 33.

The location felt like a once-in-a-lifetime chance she could not pass up. Owning a storefront salon in the center of her hometown is, in her words, a full-circle moment.
